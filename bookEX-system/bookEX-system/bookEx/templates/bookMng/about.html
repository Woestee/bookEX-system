    About Us

    Welcome to Our Book Management System

    This is a comprehensive book management platform where users can:

    • Post and share books
    • Browse a collection of books from other users
    • Add comments and ratings to books
    • Save favorite books to a personal collection
    • Manage their own book listings

    Our Mission

    We aim to create a community of book lovers who can share their favorite reads, discover new books, and connect through their shared love of literature.

    Features

    • User Authentication: Secure login and registration system
    • Book Posting: Share books with title, web link, price, and images
    • Rating System: Rate books from 1 to 5 stars
    • Comments: Leave and read comments on any book
    • Favorites: Save books to your personal favorites list
    • My Books: Manage your own posted books
